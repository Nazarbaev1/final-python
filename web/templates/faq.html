How to return the purchased ticket?

If your plans have changed, you can return the purchased ticket half an hour before the start of the session. To return a ticket purchased at the cinema box office, contact the administrator and ask for a ticket refund application form. Fill out the form and give it to the administrator along with the movie ticket and the receipt issued when buying the ticket.

Do I need to print out a ticket purchased online?

Now with electronic tickets, you can enter the hall without queuing at the checkout!

When will the schedule for tomorrow / the day after tomorrow / next week appear?

The schedule for the new week (from Thursday) opens, as a rule, on Tuesday or Wednesday morning. The weekend schedule opens, as a rule, on Friday morning.

How to go to the cinema cheaper?

Each of our cinemas has its own system of benefits. At a discount, students, schoolchildren, children, pensioners, birthday people can go to the cinema with us! And in the "Flying Days" anyone will be able to watch a movie at a special price throughout the day.
